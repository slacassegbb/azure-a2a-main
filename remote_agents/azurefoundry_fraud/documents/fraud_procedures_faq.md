# Fraud Detection Procedures & FAQs

_Last updated: 2025-09-24 | Version 3.0_

---

## 1. Workflow
1. Review incoming claim.  
2. Check domain-specific fraud indicators.  
3. Compare against cost/estimation data.  
4. Flag anomalies with explanation.  
5. Escalate to SIU when thresholds hit.  
6. Log reasoning in file.  

---

## 2. Escalation Rules
- Auto: staged collision suspected.  
- Property: intentional fire or inflated contents.  
- Travel: forged documents.  
- Health: phantom billing.  

---

## 3. Standards
- Do not accuse insured → flag internally.  
- Record specific evidence (receipts, reports, timestamps).  
- Provide supporting context (not assumptions).  

---

## 4. FAQs
- **Does fraud detection deny claims?** No, only flags for review.  
- **What if suspicion is wrong?** Claim continues, flag removed.  
- **How many audits?** ≥10% of closed claims monthly.  
- **How to handle repeat offenders?** Document + auto-escalate to SIU.  

---

## 5. Case Study
- **Fraudulent – Travel Illness Forgery:**  
  - Fake doctor note for cancellation.  
  - Signature inconsistent with clinic records.  
  - Social posts show traveler abroad.  
  - → Escalated, denied.  

- **Legitimate – Auto Theft:**  
  - Keys + GPS logs + police report provided.  
  - Car recovered stripped, damages $12k.  
  - → Paid in full.
