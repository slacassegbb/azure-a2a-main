# Universal Fraud Detection Reference

_Last updated: 2025-09-24 | Version 3.0_

---

## 1. General Red Flags
- Claim filed soon after policy start or increase.  
- Multiple claims in short time.  
- Reporting delayed >30 days.  
- Vague/changing story.  
- Witness conflicts.  

---

## 2. Anomaly Patterns
- Cluster of similar claims in one area (fraud ring).  
- Same contractor/provider across many claims.  
- Invoices inflated beyond market.  
- Identical documents reused.  

---

## 3. SIU Referral Triggers
- Suspected intentional acts.  
- High-value claims (> $50k) poorly documented.  
- Fraud signs across >1 domain.  
- 2+ red flags triggered.  

---

## 4. Case Studies
- **Fraudulent – Contractor Fraud Ring:**  
  - 5 water backup claims, same street/contractor.  
  - Invoices inflated 2–3x.  
  - → SIU found organized fraud.  

- **Legitimate – Natural Disaster:**  
  - Hailstorm → 50+ claims in 2 days.  
  - Weather confirmed, damage consistent.  
  - → Paid.
