# Property Fraud Detection Reference

_Last updated: 2025-09-24 | Version 3.0_

---

## 1. Common Fraud Indicators
- Fire shortly after policy inception or coverage increase.  
- Multiple prior claims for water backup.  
- Contents claimed without receipts/photos.  
- Contractor estimates well above market.  
- Witness/neighbors report conflicting stories.  

---

## 2. Known Property Schemes
- **Overstated Contents:** Luxury goods listed without proof.  
- **Accelerated Wear:** Normal aging roof claimed as sudden loss.  
- **Intentional Fire:** Evidence of accelerants, financial distress.  
- **Phantom Burglary:** No forced entry.  

---

## 3. Behavioral Red Flags
- Insured pushes for immediate payout.  
- Refuses inspection.  
- Story changes when retold.  

---

## 4. Escalation Triggers
- Loss >$50k with weak documentation.  
- Fire within 90 days of new coverage.  
- Contents claim > dwelling rebuild cost.  

---

## 5. Case Studies
- **Fraudulent – Intentional Fire:**  
  - Coverage boosted $150k two months before fire.  
  - Accelerant found in debris.  
  - Insured behind on mortgage.  
  - → SIU confirmed arson → denied.  

- **Legitimate – Burst Pipe:**  
  - Plumber report + photos match weather data.  
  - Damages $22k, receipts valid.  
  - → Paid.
