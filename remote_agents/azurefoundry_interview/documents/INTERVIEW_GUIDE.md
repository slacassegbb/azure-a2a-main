## What We Look For in Clients

### Ideal Client Profile
- Clear business problem that AI can address
- Executive sponsorship and budget authority
- Access to relevant data (or willingness to collect it)
- Realistic expectations about AI capabilities
- Commitment to change management and adoption

### Red Flags
- Unclear problem definition or "AI for AI's sake"
- No budget allocated or unrealistic budget expectations
- Lack of data or unwillingness to invest in data quality
- Expectation of 100% accuracy or "magic" solutions
- No stakeholder alignment or change management plan

## Qualification Criteria

A well-qualified lead typically has:

✅ **Clear Business Problem**: Specific pain point or opportunity that AI can address  
✅ **Technical Readiness**: Some data infrastructure and technical capabilities  
✅ **Budget Alignment**: Realistic budget of $50,000+ for meaningful projects  
✅ **Timeline Clarity**: Specific timeframe or business driver  
✅ **Decision Authority**: Access to decision-makers and budget owners  
✅ **Cultural Fit**: Openness to innovation and change  
✅ **Data Availability**: Relevant data exists or can be collected  

## Next Steps After Qualification

Depending on the client's readiness:

1. **High-Fit, Ready Now**: Schedule discovery workshop with senior consultants
2. **Good-Fit, Needs Education**: Provide case studies and schedule exploratory call
3. **Promising, But Premature**: Nurture relationship, provide resources, follow up quarterly
4. **Poor Fit**: Politely decline or refer to alternative resources

## Common Questions & Answers

**Q: How long does a typical AI project take?**  
A: It depends on scope, but expect 1-3 months for proof-of-concept, 3-6 months for production deployment.

**Q: What if we don't have much data?**  
A: We can help you with data strategy, work with pre-trained models, or start with a data collection phase.

**Q: How do we measure ROI?**  
A: We establish clear KPIs upfront - typically time savings, cost reduction, revenue increase, or quality improvement.

**Q: Do we need AI/ML expertise on our team?**  
A: Not initially, but we recommend building internal capabilities over time. We provide training and knowledge transfer.

**Q: What about security and compliance?**  
A: We follow industry best practices and can work within your security framework. We're experienced with GDPR, HIPAA, SOC2, etc.

**Q: Can you integrate with our existing systems?**  
A: Yes, we specialize in integration. We work with APIs, databases, and enterprise systems.

## Interview Best Practices

### Do's
✓ Ask open-ended questions to understand the full context  
✓ Listen actively and probe deeper based on responses  
✓ Show genuine curiosity about their business  
✓ Acknowledge pain points and validate concerns  
✓ Use industry terminology appropriately  
✓ Share relevant success stories when applicable  
✓ Set clear expectations about process and timeline  

### Don'ts
✗ Overwhelm with too many questions at once  
✗ Use jargon that might confuse the customer  
✗ Make promises about specific outcomes  
✗ Rush through qualification to get to next steps  
✗ Assume you understand their problem without probing  
✗ Dismiss concerns about AI limitations  
✗ Be pushy about moving forward if fit isn't clear  

## Contact & Follow-Up

After a successful qualification interview:

1. **Summarize** what you learned about their needs
2. **Confirm** key details (timeline, budget, stakeholders)
3. **Suggest** appropriate next steps based on their readiness
4. **Schedule** follow-up or consultation if applicable
5. **Capture** all information in CRM for sales team follow-up
