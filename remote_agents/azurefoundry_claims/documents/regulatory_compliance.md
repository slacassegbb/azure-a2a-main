## 1. Fair Claims Handling Principles

- **Timeliness:** Acknowledge FNOL within 1 business day.  
- **Communication:** Update insured every 10 business days or sooner.  
- **Decision:** Approve or deny claim within 30 calendar days of documentation.  
- **Payment:** Issue within 5 business days of agreement.  

---

## 2. Documentation & Transparency

- All coverage decisions must cite policy provisions.  
- Denial letters must include appeal/complaint procedures.  
- Claim file must show rationale for settlement calculations.  
- Redact unnecessary PII before sharing externally.  

---

## 3. Common Compliance Pitfalls

- **Bad Faith Exposure:** Delaying settlement without valid reason.  
- **Improper Denial:** Denying claim without citing policy exclusion.  
- **Missed Deadlines:** Not meeting state-mandated claim decision timelines.  
- **Failure to Escalate:** Ignoring red flags or customer complaints.  

---

## 4. Escalation Triggers

- **Unresolved > 30 days:** Escalate to team lead.  
- **Unresolved > 45 days:** Compliance/legal review.  
- **High-Value (> $50k):** Mandatory manager sign-off.  
- **Fraud Indicators:** Immediate referral to SIU.  

---

## 5. Sample Compliance Timelines (US Benchmarks)

- **Texas:** 15 days to acknowledge, 15 days to request info, 15 days to accept/deny.  
- **California:** 15 days to acknowledge, 40 days to accept/deny, 30 days to pay.  
- **New York:** 15 business days to acknowledge, 30 days to accept/deny.  

---

## 6. Complaint Handling

- Provide written response to customer complaints within 15 business days.  
- Escalate unresolved complaints to compliance department.  
- Maintain logs of all complaints for regulatory review.  

---

## 7. Best Practices

- Keep templates for denial and approval letters citing policy language.  
- Audit 10% of closed claims monthly for compliance.  
- Train adjusters annually on evolving regulations.
