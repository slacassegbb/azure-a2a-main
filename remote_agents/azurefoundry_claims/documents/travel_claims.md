# Travel Insurance – Claims Specialist Reference

_Last updated: 2025-09-24 | Version: 1.0_

---

## 1. Coverages

| Code | Coverage | What It Covers | Limit | Deductible |
|------|----------|----------------|-------|------------|
| TRIP-CAN | Trip Cancellation | Non-refundable prepaid costs | $10k | $0 |
| TRIP-INT | Emergency Medical | Urgent medical & evacuation abroad | $250k | $100 |
| TRIP-BAG | Baggage | Lost/delayed baggage, essentials | $2k loss / $300 delay | $0 |

---

## 2. Covered Reasons (Partial)

- Serious illness/injury (with physician certification).  
- Severe weather preventing departure.  
- Jury duty or court summons.  
- Dwelling uninhabitable (fire, flood, HO claim filed).  
- Terrorist incident at destination within 30 days of travel.  

---

## 3. Exclusions

- Pre-existing conditions not disclosed.  
- Elective/cosmetic procedures abroad.  
- Losses due to intoxication or unlawful acts.  
- Travel to sanctioned countries.  
- Ordinary wear/delay <6 hours for baggage.  

---

## 4. Documentation

- Cancellation: Doctor’s note, airline cancellation proof, receipts.  
- Medical: Hospital invoices, medical records, discharge summary.  
- Baggage: Airline claim form, receipts for delayed essentials.  

---

## 5. Example Settlements

### Trip Cancellation
- Trip cost: $4,800  
- Reason: Illness, doctor’s certification provided.  
- Deductible: $0  
→ Pay $4,800.  

### Baggage Delay
- Delay: 48 hours.  
- Essentials purchased: $280.  
- Limit: $300.  
→ Pay $280.  

### Emergency Medical Abroad
- Hospital stay: $12,000.  
- Evacuation flight: $40,000.  
- Total: $52,000  
- Deductible: $100  
→ Pay $51,900.
