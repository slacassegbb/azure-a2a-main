## 1. Core Coverages

| Code | Coverage | What It Covers | Limit | Cost Sharing |
|------|----------|----------------|-------|--------------|
| HLTH-HOSP | Inpatient Hospitalization | Room/board, surgery, anesthesia | Policy annual max | Deductible + coinsurance |
| HLTH-OUTP | Outpatient Services | ER, urgent care, diagnostic imaging | Policy annual max | Deductible + copay |
| HLTH-RX | Prescription Drugs | Generic, brand, specialty tiers | Tiered formulary | Copay per tier |
| HLTH-PREV | Preventive Services | Annual check-ups, vaccines | 100% covered | No deductible |
| HLTH-MAT | Maternity | Pre/post-natal, delivery | Policy-specific | Deductible + coinsurance |
| HLTH-MH | Mental Health | Therapy, inpatient psychiatric | Annual max 30 visits | Deductible + copay |

---

## 2. Cost-Sharing Basics

- **Deductible:** Fixed annual amount customer pays before insurer shares costs.  
- **Coinsurance:** Percentage split after deductible (e.g., 80/20).  
- **Copay:** Fixed amount per service (e.g., $25 doctor visit).  
- **Out-of-Pocket Maximum (OOP):** After reaching, insurer pays 100% covered costs.  

---

## 3. Documentation Required

- **Hospital:** Admission/discharge notes, itemized bills, physician statement.  
- **Outpatient:** Visit notes, diagnostic codes (ICD-10), billing codes (CPT).  
- **Pharmacy:** Prescription receipts, dosage, prescriber.  
- **Maternity:** OB/GYN records, delivery summary.  
- **Mental Health:** Treatment plan, session notes, provider license.  

---

## 4. Exclusions

- Cosmetic or elective surgery.  
- Experimental or unapproved treatments.  
- Non-prescription OTC drugs (unless mandated).  
- Services from non-licensed providers.  
- Injuries from illegal activity.  

---

## 5. Example Settlements

### Hospitalization
- Total Bill: $12,000  
- Deductible: $1,500  
- Coinsurance: 80/20 until OOP met.  
→ Patient pays $1,500 + 20% of ($12,000−$1,500) = $2,100.  
→ Insurer pays $8,400.  

### Prescription
- Specialty drug: $3,000/month  
- Copay tier: $250  
→ Insured pays $250, insurer pays $2,750.  

---

## 6. Special Scenarios

- **Emergency Out-of-Network:** Covered as in-network if life-threatening.  
- **Pre-Authorization Required:** MRI, CT, inpatient stays > 2 days.  
- **Coordination of Benefits:** If patient has dual coverage, primary/secondary rules apply.
