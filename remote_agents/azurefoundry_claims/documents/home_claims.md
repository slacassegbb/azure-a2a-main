# Home/Property Insurance – Claims Specialist Reference

_Last updated: 2025-09-24 | Version: 1.0_

---

## 1. Coverages

| Code | Coverage | What It Covers | Limit | Deductible | Notes |
|------|----------|----------------|-------|------------|-------|
| HO-DW | Dwelling (A) | Structure, attached fixtures | Policy limit | $1,500 | Replacement cost if endorsement purchased |
| HO-PP | Personal Property (C) | Contents: furniture, clothing, electronics | 70% of A | $1,500 | Special limits apply |
| HO-LU | Loss of Use (D) | Additional living expenses | 20% of A | $0 | Covers hotel, meals, transport |
| HO-PL | Personal Liability (E) | Injury/damage to others | $500k | $0 | — |
| HO-MP | Medical Payments (F) | No-fault guest medical | $5k | $0 | — |
| HO-WTR | Water Backup | Sump/sewer backup | $10k | $1,000 | Separate from flood |
| HO-FLD | Flood | Flood damage | NFIP/endorsement | Policy-specific | Not in standard HO |

---

## 2. Depreciation Guidelines

- **Electronics:** 5 years  
- **Appliances:** 10 years  
- **Furniture:** 12 years  
- **Clothing:** 3 years  
- **Roof (shingle):** 20 years  

---

## 3. Documentation

- Fire: Fire department report, contractor estimates, receipts.  
- Water backup: Plumber’s report, itemized damaged property.  
- Theft: Police report, inventory list, proof of ownership.  
- Wind/hail: Weather report, contractor inspection, photos.  

---

## 4. Exclusions

- Flood (unless HO-FLD purchased).  
- Earthquake (unless endorsement).  
- Wear and tear, mold, termites.  
- Intentional acts or neglect.  
- War, nuclear hazard.  

---

## 5. Example Settlements

### Fire Loss – Contents
- Couch (RCV $2,400, age 6 years, life 12 years) → Depreciation 50% → ACV $1,200.  
- If replacement cost policy: $1,200 now, $1,200 recoverable with receipts.  

### Water Backup
- Endorsement limit: $10,000.  
- Plumber invoice: $1,800.  
- Contents loss: $6,500.  
- Deductible: $1,000.  
→ Total payable = ($1,800 + $6,500) − $1,000 = $7,300.  

---

## 6. Additional Living Expenses (ALE)

- Triggered when home certified uninhabitable.  
- Covers hotel, meals beyond normal, temporary transport.  
- Max 12 months or 20% of Coverage A.  
- Requires receipts and comparison to normal household spend.  

---

## 7. Special Scenarios

- **Jewelry Theft:** Special limit $5k unless scheduled.  
- **Electronics Limit:** $3k per item unless scheduled.  
- **Roof Replacement:** If >20 years, may be settled at ACV unless endorsed.
