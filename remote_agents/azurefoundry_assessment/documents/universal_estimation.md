# Universal Estimation Guidelines

_Last updated: 2025-09-24 | Version 3.0_

---

## 1. Workflow
1. Inspect & document damage.  
2. Identify items & scope.  
3. Apply cost tables (labor, materials).  
4. Adjust for depreciation (ACV).  
5. Apply regional multiplier.  
6. Assign severity.  
7. Report estimate.  

---

## 2. Severity Matrix
| Severity | Cost | Examples |
|----------|------|----------|
| Minor | <$5k | Broken window, bumper scuff |
| Moderate | $5k–$50k | Kitchen fire, hailstorm |
| Severe | >$50k | House fire, flood total loss |

---

## 3. Depreciation Rules
- Electronics: −20%/yr (floor 20% of RCV).  
- Furniture: −8%/yr.  
- Clothing: −33%/yr.  
- Vehicles: 1.5%/month from in-service, floor 20% MSRP.  

---

## 4. Regional Multipliers
- Metro: +20% labor.  
- Rural: −15% labor.  
- Post-disaster surge: +25–50%.  

---

## 5. Sample Report
**Assessment Report**  
- Loss Type: Fire  
- Items: Kitchen (cabinets, appliances, wiring)  
- Labor: $12,000  
- Materials: $15,000  
- Total: $27,000  
- Severity: Moderate
