# Travel Damage & Cost Estimation

_Last updated: 2025-09-24 | Version 3.0_

---

## 1. Trip Costs
- Domestic: $1,200–$3,000/person.  
- International: $3,500–$6,000/person.  
- Cruise: $2,500–$5,000/person.  
- Airline rebooking: $200–$800 (domestic), $500–$1,500 (intl).
