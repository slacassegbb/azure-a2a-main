---

## 3. Severity Levels
- **Minor:** <$5k, small leak, cosmetic smoke damage.  
- **Moderate:** $5k–$50k, multi-room flood/fire.  
- **Severe:** >$50k, structural compromise, unsafe.  

---

## 4. Examples
- **Basement flood (12” water):** $18,500.  
- **Kitchen fire (cabinets, appliances, wiring):** $27,000.  
- **Windstorm (30% roof):** $9,500.  
- **Contents loss (TV + laptop):** $2,200 replacement, $1,500 ACV.  
- **Wildfire smoke damage (whole house clean + repaint):** $45,000.
